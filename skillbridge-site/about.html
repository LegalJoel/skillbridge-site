Our Story

SkillBridge Liberia was born from a simple observation: Liberia is full of talented, hard-working tradespeople — electricians, plumbers, welders, carpenters, masons — who have the skills but lack the platform, the clients, and the professional framework to thrive.

At the same time, homeowners, businesses, and construction companies are desperately searching for reliable, trustworthy workers and repeatedly getting burned by no-shows, shoddy work, and zero accountability.

We built SkillBridge Liberia to fix both sides of that problem — by creating a verified, safety-trained, professionally managed network of skilled workers who are dispatched directly to clients who need them.

Founded in 2026 and officially registered with the Liberia Business Registry (TIN: 502350109), SkillBridge Liberia is aligned with the government's AITB Strategic Plan 2025–2029 and fully compliant with OHS Regulation No. 19.

🎯 Our Mission

To organise, empower, and professionally develop Liberia's skilled trades workforce by providing registration, safety training, and job placement services.

🚀 Our Vision

A Liberia where every skilled tradesperson has dignified, consistent work — and every client has access to reliable, certified, professional service.
