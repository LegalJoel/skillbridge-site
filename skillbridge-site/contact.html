Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to what clients and workers ask us most

How quickly can you send a worker?

We respond within 2 hours of your request and dispatch a worker within 24 hours. For emergencies, we prioritise same-day dispatch.

How do I pay?

You pay the worker directly (cash or Orange Money/MTN). SkillBridge charges a 10–15% coordination fee on the job value, also payable via mobile money.

What if I'm not happy with the work?

We have a Quality Replacement Guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the work, we'll send a replacement worker at no additional cost. Just call us within 24 hours.

How do I register as a worker?

Fill out the worker registration form on our Register page, or WhatsApp us directly. We'll verify your references and invite you to a safety training session.

Do you work outside Monrovia?

Currently we operate in Monrovia and nearby areas. We're expanding to all 15 counties of Liberia by 2028. Contact us if you need services in other locations.

Can companies hire multiple workers?

Yes. We offer B2B services including on-demand workforce pools, monthly retainers, and project-based deployment. Contact us for a custom quote.
