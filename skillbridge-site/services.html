Electrical Work

Our verified electricians handle residential, commercial, and light industrial electrical work across Monrovia. All electricians carry their SkillBridge ID card and complete our OHS electrical safety module before dispatch.

Common jobs include: New wiring and rewiring, circuit breaker installation & repair, power socket & switch installation, solar panel wiring, generator connections & transfer switches, outdoor/security lighting, ceiling fan installation, electrical fault diagnosis.

ResidentialCommercialSolar SystemsGenerator WorkEmergency Repairs

Plumbing

Reliable plumbers for homes, offices, and construction sites. Our plumbers are trained in basic pipe sizing, pressure systems, and water sanitation safety.

Common jobs include: Water pipe installation & repair, bathroom & kitchen fitting, water tank installation & repair, drainage & sewer systems, borehole pump installation, leak detection & repair, toilet, sink & shower repairs, water meter installation.

ResidentialCommercialLeak RepairsTank & PumpNew Construction

Welding & Metal Fabrication

Skilled welders for construction, security, and custom metalwork. Workers complete our fire & burn prevention safety module as a mandatory requirement.

Common jobs include: Security gates & burglar bars, fence & perimeter fabrication, roof truss & metal structure work, staircase & railing fabrication, vehicle body repairs, custom metal furniture, storage tanks & containers, structural steel repairs.

Security WorksConstruction SteelCustom FabricationVehicle Work

Carpentry & Woodwork

Experienced carpenters for furniture, construction fit-out, and home improvements. All carry basic hand tool and power tool safety certification.

Common jobs include: Custom furniture making, door & window frame installation, ceiling & suspended ceilings, kitchen cabinets & wardrobes, roofing timber work, wood floor laying, renovation carpentry, office partitioning & interiors.

Custom FurnitureRoofingInterior Fit-OutRenovations

Masonry & Construction

Qualified masons for building, renovation, and civil construction work. Workers are trained in fall prevention, scaffolding safety, and material handling.

Common jobs include: Block laying & bricklaying, concrete mixing & casting, floor & wall tiling, plastering & rendering, foundation work, drainage channels, renovation & demolition, retaining walls & compound walls.

FoundationsBlock LayingTilingPlasteringRenovations

General Repairs & Maintenance

Flexible handyman services for homes, offices, and commercial properties. Ideal for property managers, landlords, expats, and businesses needing regular maintenance support.

Common jobs include: AC servicing & repairs, painting (interior & exterior), appliance repairs, general home maintenance, roof repairs, gutter cleaning & repair, door lock & window repairs, property inspection & punch-list execution.

Home MaintenanceAC ServicePaintingProperty Management
